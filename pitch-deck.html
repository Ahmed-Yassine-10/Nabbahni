03 · Innovation
Pourquoi ?
لماذا؟
Unique twist

Demand censoring correction

Stockout days record fake-low sales. We impute them from a 14-day trailing average — so shortages don't teach the model that demand fell. Skip this and the AI under-orders exactly the medicines that matter.

Method

Global quantile models

One LightGBM per horizon — not per product — predicting P10 / P50 / P90: a forecast plus its own confidence interval, robust even for rare medicines.

Architecture

Explain at write time

SHAP runs during nightly scoring, never in the request path. Explanations are pre-written, stored, and free at read time.

Deliberate

Rules, not an LLM

Recommendations are deterministic: deficit = forecast × 1.2 − stock − in-transit. A regulator can recompute every number. Plus a hard override: essential + <5 days cover → critical.
