# Launch Strategy

## Positioning
**SentinelleRx — la météo des médicaments de Tunisie.** A public-good early-warning
system that lets the health system act *before* a shortage reaches patients, with
transparent, professionally-validated recommendations.

## Stakeholders & value
| Stakeholder | Value proposition |
|---|---|
| PCT | National visibility, earlier procurement, quantified financial impact of actions. |
| Ministry of Health | Real-time national situational awareness; evidence for policy. |
| Regional authorities | Governorate-level risk view; targeted redistribution. |
| Hospital & community pharmacies | Local alerts, recommended orders, validated substitutions. |
| Suppliers/importers | Demand signals to plan capacity and reduce delays. |
| Citizens | Find where a medicine is available; discover validated alternatives. |

## Go-to-market phases
1. **Institutional partnership** — MoU with PCT + Ministry; governance and data
   sharing agreements; DPIA.
2. **Pilot (Grand Tunis)** — 10–20 pharmacies + PCT; validate accuracy and
   workflows; collect professional feedback.
3. **Clinical & procurement validation** — pharmacists and PCT officers confirm
   that explanations and recommendations are trustworthy and actionable.
4. **Regional expansion** — Sfax, Sousse; refine models on real data.
5. **National launch** — all governorates; citizen portal public campaign.

## Trust & adoption levers
- **Explainability everywhere** — no black-box alerts; every risk shows its "why".
- **Human-in-the-loop** — the platform advises; professionals decide.
- **Bilingual, mobile-first citizen access** (French + Arabic, RTL).
- **Transparency** — model performance reports (`model_comparison.md`) shared with
  the PCT; open aggregate-availability data.

## Communication plan
- Launch with the Ministry + PCT; press on "predicting shortages before they hit".
- Onboarding materials for pharmacists (short guides + in-app help).
- Citizen awareness via health centers, pharmacy posters, social media.

## KPIs post-launch
- % of shortages predicted ≥ 7 days in advance.
- Reduction in stockout-days for essential medications YoY.
- Recommendation validation rate + realized procurement lead-time improvement.
- Citizen portal usage (searches, unique users) and satisfaction.

## Risks & mitigations
| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Data quality from heterogeneous pharmacy systems | Robust ETL validation + quarantine; phased onboarding. |
| Professional distrust of AI | Explainability + human validation + transparent metrics. |
| Over-reliance on predictions | Clear "decision-support only" framing; confidence intervals shown. |
| Privacy concerns | Anonymous citizen use; hashed contacts; DPIA; data minimization. |
